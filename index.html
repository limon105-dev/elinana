What is Elinana?

Elinana means "highly favoured"—because every child is elevated in grace. We create wearable reminders of God's love, with each garment telling the story of a spiritual champion who points children back to the source of all grace, hope, and courage.

Our premium children's apparel combines beautiful design with meaningful storytelling, giving parents tools to spark conversations about faith, character, and God's unending love for their little ones.

Elinana is a Hebrew word meaning 'highly favoured.' We chose this name because every child is elevated in grace and deeply loved by God. It's pronounced 'El-lee Na-na.'
