Why 'Elinana '?

Pronounced: eh-LEE-nah-nah

eh • LEE • nah • nah

Elinana comes from Hebrew roots:

Eli = "high" or "elevated"

Nana = "favour" or "grace"

Together meaning "highly favoured"

We believe every child is elevated in grace, surrounded by love, and born with God-given purpose.

Our Mission

To create tools for parents that spark conversations about faith, character, and God's boundless love.

Each Elinana garment is designed to be a conversation starter—a tangible way to discuss biblical truths and character in everyday moments.

Our Commitment to Giving

As an act of worship and obedience, we tithe 10% of all Elinana proceeds to support local churches, global missions, and charities that serve vulnerable children and families worldwide.

Your purchase directly fuels this outreach.
